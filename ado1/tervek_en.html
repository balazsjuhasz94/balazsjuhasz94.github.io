Future Plans

A few ideas for potential future improvements:

  • English version — for foreigners living in Hungary, and because it would be fun. (Translating organization goals and everything else with an LLM.)
  • Organization websites — it would be possible to automatically search for organizations' websites online and make them clickable. Taking it further, it would be interesting to have a single transparent place where the activities of each organization are compiled and easily accessible.
  • Analysis of excluded organizations — there are 960 organizations that failed to account for their donations last year and were therefore excluded this year. It would be interesting to find trends in which categories this is most common.
  • Covering all organizations — the current analysis is based on 10,000 out of 37,000 organizations. The ones left out are typically chosen by fewer than 20 people — mostly very locally active organizations. It would be interesting to see the full picture one day. The visualization challenges are real, but it wouldn't be too costly to iterate over the remainder — roughly 10,000–12,000 HUF at similar settings.
